Common Questions

Is remote access secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard 256-bit encryption. You can see everything we do on your screen, and we only have access while the session is active.

What if you can't fix my issue?

We operate on a "No Fix, No Fee" policy for our incident-based support. If we can't resolve it, you don't pay.

Do you support Macs?

Yes! We support Windows, macOS, and Linux systems, as well as iOS and Android mobile devices.

How quickly can you start?

For most incidents, we can connect within 5-10 minutes of your ticket submission or call.
